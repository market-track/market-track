Frequently Asked Questions

What does Market Track do?

Market Track lets you monitor cryptocurrency prices, charts, and your portfolio in real time across thousands of assets.

Does the app require registration?

No. Market Track does not require you to create an account or sign in.

Where is my data stored?

All user-created data (portfolios, watchlists, preferences) is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers to store this data. However, the App uses third-party analytics services (Google Firebase) which collect pseudonymous technical and usage data to improve app performance and stability. See our Privacy Policy for details.

The price data looks incorrect or delayed. Why?

Market data is sourced from third-party providers and may be delayed or differ from broker prices. The App is for informational purposes only. Always rely on your broker's official data for trading decisions.

The app crashes or doesn't load. What should I do?

  • Close and reopen the app
  • Make sure you have a stable internet connection
  • Update to the latest version from the App Store or Google Play
  • Restart your device
  • If the issue persists, contact us

How do I delete all my data?

You can clear all locally stored data directly within the app via the in-app data clearing option in the menu. Alternatively, uninstalling the app from your device will also remove all locally stored data.

Is the app available on other platforms?

Yes. Market Track is available on both iOS and Android:

  • App Store
  • Google Play
